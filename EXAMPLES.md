## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Natural**: Don't overthink - just chat normally
2. **Train Well**: More diverse training = better personality
3. **Enable Auto-Learn**: Let relationships grow organically
4. **Be Patient**: Relationships develop over conversations
5. **Stay Consistent**: Regular interactions build stronger bonds
6. **Mix It Up**: Different topics create richer personas

---

**Remember**: The more you interact, the better the AI understands you and adjusts its personality!
